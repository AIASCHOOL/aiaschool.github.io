Policy regarding personal information protection

Aichi International Academy (AIA) strives to provide education-related services with the core businesses of "Japanese language education business" and "study abroad business."

In promoting these business activities, we are deeply aware that it is extremely important to appropriately handle and protect the personal information of our customers and other people related to our group. AIA has established the following personal information protection policy, and declares that it will be implemented and maintained by thoroughly disseminating the policy to our officers, employees, etc.

1. Regarding personal information handled in all businesses and the personal information of employees, etc., we will comply with laws and regulations regarding the handling of personal information, guidelines established by the government, and other norms.

2. Personal information will be collected, used, provided, etc. by lawful and fair means to the extent necessary for business purposes, and used and provided within the scope of the purpose. If it becomes necessary to handle personal information beyond the scope of the specified purpose of use, we will notify the person of the purpose of use and obtain their consent, and we will not use the information for any other purpose.

3. For all personal information handled within AIA, we will take reasonable safety measures and corrective measures against risks such as unauthorized access, loss, destruction, falsification, leakage, etc.

4. AIA will sincerely and promptly respond to requests for disclosure, correction, deletion, suspension of use, etc. of personal information, as well as complaints and consultations, from individuals.

5. We will appropriately maintain a system for protecting personal information and continually improve it in light of changes in the situation, social trends regarding personal information protection, customer requests, etc.

Heisei International Educational Development Co., Ltd.
