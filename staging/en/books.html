Educational Materials and Tools Development

To measure the learning outcomes of students, our institute has established a research group in collaboration with internal faculty and affiliated universities, dedicated to developing unique educational materials and tools. We have experience as a cooperative school for material development with the Japanese Language Education Promotion Association, having edited and published the Japanese Study Exam Mock Test Series and numerous other reference books. Particularly, we emphasize the development and adoption of teaching materials and tools that can apply cutting-edge technologies
Introduction of the Audio Pen
This institution has partnered with Taiwan's major publisher of Japanese language education-related books, 'Da Xin Bookstore,' and actively introduced teaching materials and tools that can be utilized with audio pens
