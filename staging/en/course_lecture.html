EJU Special Lecture
  This course is an advanced class designed to strengthen subjects beyond the regular curriculum hours for university entrance exams, such as EJU. It focuses on subjects including mathematics, physics, chemistry, biology, and comprehensive subjects, in addition to comprehension, listening, listening and reading comprehension, and essay writing, which are part of the EJU exam subjects. The goal is to prepare students for admission to national or prestigious private universities.

　➀ The course shares a similar purpose with the advanced classes in Japanese senior high schools.
　➁ For those who choose to enroll in the entire course, we offer a bonus system that includes free repeated attendance, support for application document preparation, and interview practice.
　➂ For those opting to take the entire course, we provide a bonus system, including free repeated attendance, assistance with application document preparation, and interview practice.
　④ Classes are conducted in small groups to facilitate personalized instruction.
　➄ Utilize the series of reference books, including the 'Simulation Questions for the Examination for Japanese University Admission for International Students,' developed by our academy to enhance the learning effectiveness even further.
Japanese Language Teacher Training Course
This course is offered in collaboration with the General Incorporated Foundation International Lifelong Learning Research Foundation for the purpose of obtaining qualifications as a Japanese language teacher.
Chinese Descendants School
This course is primarily designed to support Chinese descendants residing in the Tokai region, providing assistance in learning Japanese, Chinese, English, mathematics, and other subjects.
Specific Skills

Upon reaching a certain level of proficiency in Japanese and passing the specific skills examination in one of the 14 designated industries, individuals can apply for the new residency status introduced in April 2019. This course is designed for students who have graduated from our academy and wish to change their career path to employment.

As a registered support organization, our academy can collaborate with companies and provide support for the application of residency status changes.
