Short-term Study TourProgram

Japanese Language Lesson and Cultural Experience
In addition to knowledge gained from books, it is essential to travel to various places, broaden one's horizons, and expand one's experiences by exploring on one's own.

Japanese Language Lesson
ネイティブな日本語を学べる　To Study Native Japanese
　The short-term study tour program consists of Japanese language training and cultural experiences. The Japanese language training is designed for learners of Japanese or those who have recently developed an interest in the language and wish to come to Japan. It targets individuals who want to learn native Japanese conversation, refine their polite conversation and public speaking skills, delve into proverbs, idioms, and grammatical structures. The course content is tailored based on the preferences of the applicants.
　This short-term (within 90 days) language training and cultural experience program estimate generally includes arrangements for accommodation, meals, classes, and excursions. The emphasis is primarily on devising ways to achieve these components at a minimal cost.
Japanese Cultures Experience
日本のｸｰﾙを探求する To Discover Japanese "Cool"
In addition to Japanese language training, this course offers participants the opportunity to broaden their perspectives by experiencing various aspects of Japanese social and cultural life. Participants can freely choose from eight cultural experiences.
Educational Experience
As of the information provided in 2023, there are 2,721 higher education institutions nationwide in Japan. This includes 309 junior colleges and 807 universities. In Aichi Prefecture, there are 52 universities, 114 vocational schools, 443 high schools, 509 junior high schools, 984 elementary schools, and 490 kindergartens. Visits to schools and cultural exchanges are possible.
History Experience
The Tokai region is the birthplace of three great heroes: Tokugawa Ieyasu, Toyotomi Hideyoshi, and Oda Nobunaga. Many castles from the Warring States period still stand here. Temples imbued with cultural significance and old townscapes offer a glimpse into Japan's history as you explore the Tokai region.
Traditional Experience
Experience the lifestyle and culture of various eras in Japan. Immerse yourself in the traditional culture, performing arts, and the iconic Japanese food culture that has been passed down through generations in the Tokai region. These experiences are sure to become unforgettable memories.
Matsuri Experience
Experience the living culture of various eras in Japan. Explore the traditional culture, traditional performing arts, and the symbolic Japanese food culture that has been passed down through generations in the Tokai region. These experiences are sure to become unforgettable memories.
Nature Experience
The Tokai region offers breathtaking natural landscapes throughout the four seasons—blue seas, mountains, rivers, valleys, cherry blossoms, fresh greenery, autumn leaves, and pure white snow. Experience the ever-changing beauty of Japan's nature in the Tokai region. Feel the "essence of Japan" through the natural beauty of Tokai and the charm of each season.
Industry Experience
The Tokai region is a hub of industrial production in Japan. Tourism in the Tokai region offers the opportunity to experience automobile manufacturing factories and visit historical museums showcasing the industrial development. Additionally, you can engage in experiences such as pottery and traditional paper-making, allowing you to personally immerse in time-honored cultural traditions.
Entertainment Experience
One of the greatest pleasures of traveling in Japan is, without exaggeration, the hot springs. Hot springs surrounded by nature, such as by the river or in a valley, offer a charming experience. You can easily visit hot springs for a day trip as well. Let's heal the fatigue of the journey. Japan places a high emphasis on food safety. In addition to local cuisine, you can enjoy dishes from around the world. There are not only high-end restaurants but also plenty of chain restaurants, fast-food options, and specialty stores. Family restaurants offer a wide variety of dishes at affordable prices.
Accommodation Facilities
　The academy, aiming for economic efficiency and safety, has set up a seminar house utilizing a section of its self-owned student dormitory. This seminar house can accommodate around 20 students and is provided to short-term training participants at a low cost.
