Facilities and Equipment

School Building/ Student Dormitory/ Seminar House

School Building
   The school building consists of a self-owned 7th floor in Building 1 and a 4th floor in Building 2, totaling a site area of 739 square meters and a building area of 1,506 square meters with a reinforced concrete structure.
    Facilities include the office of the school director, a large conference room, faculty and staff rooms, administrative and academic affairs offices, 12 large classrooms, 3 small classrooms, a career guidance room, a health room, a PC corner, a multipurpose training room, a Japanese cultural experience room, a library with a PC audio reading IT pen, a music and entertainment room, 2 guest rooms, 2 roof gardens, and 3 bicycle parking spaces. The entire building is equipped with air conditioning, shower toilets, air purifiers, TVs, and other electrical facilities.

Seminar House
    In addition to the on-campus student dormitories, we have set up a seminar house within a corner of OAK Apartments where short-term exchange and study abroad students, staying for up to 90 days, can accommodate up to 20 individuals. The seminar house includes six Japanese-style rooms, a common area for socializing, separate male and female bathrooms, toilets with musical features, automatic washing machines, air conditioning, and a garden.
