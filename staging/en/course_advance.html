Advanced Education Course

Graduate school・ College ・ Junior college ・ Vocational school

Educational Goals
To cultivate global talent who can thrive on the world stage
Targeting international students who aim to advance to Japanese vocational schools, universities, and graduate schools, we develop comprehensive Japanese language skills as a foundation for learning and research. Through our curriculum, students acquire the general knowledge, customs, and cultural literacy essential for life in Japanese society, and become well-rounded individuals with a global perspective who can contribute to both Japanese and international communities.
