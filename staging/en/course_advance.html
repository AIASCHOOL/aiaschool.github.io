Advanced Education Course

Graduate school・ College ・ Junior college ・ Vocational school

Education Objectives
In cultivating international talent, striving for success on the world stage.
For foreign students aiming to enter Japanese vocational schools, universities, and graduate schools, comprehensive Japanese language skills are developed, serving as a foundation for their learning and research. Additionally, for foreigners aspiring to work in Japanese companies, necessary Japanese language skills for the workplace are cultivated, along with knowledge of Japanese society, customs, and culture, thereby nurturing a broad range of talents with an international perspective who can contribute to society both within and outside of Japan.
Course Guide
Spring term students starting in April have a two-year program, while autumn term students starting in October have a one-and-a-half-year program.
・ 2-Year Advanced Education Course (Enrollment in April, Graduation in March two years later)
・ 1.5-Year Advanced Education Course (Enrollment in October, Graduation in March two years later)

Course Features
■ Excellent instructors ■ Detailed college guidance ■ Plentiful recommended admission destinations ■ Splendid school events ■ Well-equipped dormitories
Course Syllabus
Prepare classes separated into lectures and exercises, and a variety of strategy courses.
Mata pelajaran wajib
Japanese language classes including grammar, exercises, conversation, listening comprehension, reading comprehension, and composition (including preparation for the Japanese-Language Proficiency Test and academic guidance

Career Path Selection
After graduating from this institute, there are various career options available.
Specialist School and Junior College Enrollment
Passers of the Japanese Language Proficiency Test (JLPT) N3 or higher
University and Graduate School Enrollment
Passing the Japanese Language Proficiency Test (JLPT) N2 Level or Higher for Foreigners
Employment in a Japanese Company
Foreigners who have passed the Japanese Language Proficiency Test (JLPT) N1 or above and have graduated from a university in their home country
Working in labor for a Japanese Company
Foreigners who have passed the Japanese Language Proficiency Test (JLPT) N4 or above, and the Work Skills Assessment Test

Admission Application
Eligibility for Recruitment
International Student
Eligibility for Application
❶ Those who have completed 12 years or more of school education through the regular education curriculum in their home country, or those who are recognized to have equivalent academic abilities.
❷ Those who are in good physical and mental health, have a strong desire to study, and have the financial capability to cover the expenses of studying abroad.
Period of Application
2-Year Advanced Study Course (Starting in April): Application deadline is November 20th each year (Submission deadline to the Immigration Bureau is February early).
1.5-Year Academic Course (October Enrollment) - Application Deadline: May 20th Annually (Submission to the Immigration Bureau Deadline: Mid-June)
Please note that if the capacity is reached, the application may close earlier than scheduled.
Residence Status
After a student applies, our institute applies to the Japan Immigration Services Agency for the issuance of a student visa. The first visa is valid for a period of one year and three months, with the possibility of extending it for a second period of study.
Application Procedure
Please apply by attaching either the applicant or a representative, an application form, a contact form, a copy of the passport, a copy of the ID card, or a birth certificate, and submit it via email, mail, or directly to the school's office.
